## Perception
- Since birth, cognitive model develops continuously from information is gathered from environment in recurrent process. Refer to simplified subdiagram. 
- Information learned in a specific iteration can be used to analyze incoming information in subsequent iterations.
- Information perception involves below functions: 
  - Emotions:
    - Realtime emotional response to incoming data/experiences.
  - Cognitive biases
    - Normal bias, Baseline bias, Association bias, Identity biases
  - [Reward system](/essays/reward_system)
  - Learned information
    - Capability of using existing information, skills to analyse incoming information.
    - Capable of learning and becoming aware of our own emotions and biases.
